Privacy Policy

PosturePal – Posture Reminder App

Last updated: March 18, 2025

1. Introduction

PosturePal ("we," "our," or "the app") is a posture reminder and water intake tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

PosturePal is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with external servers. All data stays on your device.

We store the following information locally on your device only:

  • App preferences: Reminder intervals, posture check schedules, and water intake goals
  • Usage data: Your posture reminder completions and daily water intake amounts
  • Settings: Theme preference (light/dark mode), language, and notification preferences
  • Dates: Used internally to reset daily/weekly counters and organize your progress

This data is stored using your device's local storage (SharedPreferences) and is never sent to our servers or any third party.

3. How We Use Your Information

Locally stored information is used solely to:

  • Display your posture and water intake progress
  • Schedule personalized reminders at the intervals you choose
  • Remember your preferences between app sessions
  • Show charts and weekly summaries

4. Permissions We Request

To provide core functionality, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): To send posture and water reminders
  • Wake Lock: To ensure background reminders fire reliably
  • Receive Boot Completed: To reschedule reminders after your device restarts
  • Vibrate: For notification alerts (optional behavior)

We do not access your contacts, location, photos, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive data.

5. Third-Party Services

PosturePal does not integrate with analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services. We do not use cookies or similar technologies. The app runs entirely on your device without sending data to external servers.

6. Data Retention

Your data is stored on your device until you uninstall the app. Uninstalling PosturePal will remove all locally stored data. We have no ability to access or retain your data after uninstallation.

7. Data Security

Because all data stays on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security (e.g., lock screen, encryption). We do not transmit your data over the internet, so it is not at risk of interception by third parties through our app.

8. Children's Privacy

PosturePal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app or on our website. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy reflects the most recent version.
